The goal

One home for AI asset creation.
No more juggling a dozen tools.

AI generation is fragmented — a different tool, account, and API for every image model, 3D service, and LLM. Asset Hub brings them together: a single platform that orchestrates every generator behind one API, stores every result in one searchable library, and enriches it automatically with LLM-generated metadata. Create, refine, organize, and ship — without ever leaving the hub.

What it does

Everything between the prompt and the asset.

Unified API

One REST endpoint orchestrates every backend. Send a request, get an asset — the hub handles routing, polling, and delivery.

Multi-provider engine

Meshy, Tripo3D, ComfyUI, Ollama and OpenAI plug in through one provider registry — cloud or fully local, side by side.

Generation pipeline

Background workers track every job from pending to preview to final — auto-downloading results and refining meshes as they land.

Semantic search

Hybrid vector + keyword search across your whole library. Find any asset by what it looks like, not just what it's called.

Collections & bulk jobs

Group assets into sets and run a whole collection through generation at once — one click turns a folder of images into 3D.

LLM metadata enrichment

Every finished asset is auto-described, tagged, and categorized by an LLM — so your library stays organized without manual work.

Auth built in

JWT sessions, personal API keys, and optional OIDC / SSO — with admin approval flows and short-lived signed asset URLs.

Self-hostable

Ships as Docker Compose — FastAPI, worker, PostgreSQL and web UI. Run it entirely on your own hardware, your data stays yours.

One library, every asset

Images, video, audio, meshes and splats live together — versioned, previewable, and downloadable from a single dashboard.

Plugs into your stack

Bring your own generators.

Cloud services and local models, orchestrated together through one provider registry.
